To switch it fully on — 3 steps

  1. Make a "Capacity Mirror" form in Kit set to single opt-in. This keeps your website signups on double opt-in (unchanged) while Mirror finishers come straight in with no confirm step. Send me the form ID and I point the wheel at it. ~1 min in Kit
  2. Build the results-email automation in Kit: trigger "subscribes to the Capacity Mirror form" → action "send the Your Capacity wheel email". The email copy is written and ready to paste. I'll walk you or Luke through it. ~10 min in Kit
  3. Send Newsletter Issue 01. It's loaded and ready in Kit as a draft — you hit send when your review's done. your call on timing

What each Mirror finisher hands you

Their email + first name, their capacity state (Peak / Steady / Conserve / Restore) and their heaviest point (Behaviour or Environment), saved as Kit fields so the results email can speak to exactly where they are. Kit dedupes on email, so a repeat visitor updates rather than duplicates.
