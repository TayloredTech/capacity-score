The year

What twelve months actually looks like.

By month three you’ll see measurable change. By month six you’ll feel it. By month twelve you’ll own it.

Weeks 1–2

Onboarding

Intake, history and a working session on your actual calendar. Your wearable and glucose monitor are set up, kits are shipped, and ExecOS goes live on your phone.

Months 1–2

The full picture

The complete diagnostic battery, all eight layers of it, from genetics and hormones through to cognitive function and biological age.

Month 2

Your blueprint

We write your Personal Performance Blueprint. Not a routine, a strategy: fuelling, sleep architecture, recovery, movement and cognitive load, designed around your data and your calendar.

Months 3–11

Recalibration

A steady cadence of strategy sessions, in person or virtual, with fortnightly digital check-ins and micro-corrections between them. Your wearable and glucose data keep the plan honest against your real trajectory, not a snapshot.

Month 6

Mid-year retest

Key markers are re-measured, so progress is verified rather than assumed, and the second half of the year is built on where you actually are.

Month 12

Wrap-up testing, verified results

The full battery runs a final time and is read against your baselines from month one. A closing session turns it into your next-year operating plan. You leave with proof, not impressions.
