The model inside

ExecOS runs the capacity wheel, live.

Your biology shapes your leadership, your leadership drives your behaviour, your behaviour builds your environment, and your environment feeds straight back into your biology. ExecOS measures all four factors continuously and reads the spin, because capacity isn’t a trait, it’s a state that changes overnight.

One read, every morning

Four states. One directive.

No dashboards to interpret before coffee. ExecOS reads the wheel and hands you the day’s state, what to spend, what to protect, in plain words.

Peak

A strong day. Spend it on the work that moves the company.

Steady

Conserve

Restore

Underneath the directive

The trend underneath the day.

A single morning read is only as honest as the data underneath it. ExecOS indexes your recovery, heart rate, sleep and activity against your own baseline and holds the trend all year, so a rough week reads as a rough week, not a new normal.

Between testing rounds, this is what keeps your plan honest: your advisors watch the same trends from their side, and micro-correct before drift becomes a quarter.
