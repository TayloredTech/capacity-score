The idea

Agendas follow the clock. Attention doesn’t.

Every conference room in the world runs on the same physiology: glucose, sleep debt, cognitive load. Almost none are designed for it.

We bring the same measurement we use with private clients into event design. Menus and timing built to keep glucose stable. Agenda architecture that follows the natural rhythm of human energy rather than the schedule. Live glucose, heart-rate variability and attention tracking, with a performance team adjusting behind the scenes. When the biology works, engagement takes care of itself.
