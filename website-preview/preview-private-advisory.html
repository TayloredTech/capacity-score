Who this is for

You’re not broken. You’re carrying more than most.

Our clients already perform at a level most people can’t touch. The question is how long they can hold it, and at what cost.

Five seats a year is not a marketing device. It’s the number of people two founders can personally measure, interpret and recalibrate without delegating any of it. Every data point is reviewed by us. Every readout happens with us. If you want a programme run by a care team, this isn’t it.

The year

What twelve months actually looks like.

By month three you’ll see measurable change. By month six you’ll feel it. By month twelve you’ll own it.

Months 1–2

The full picture

The complete diagnostic battery: genetics, hormones, metabolic health, cognitive function, gut health, glucose dynamics, biological age and wearable baselines. Everything the audit deliberately leaves on the shelf.

Month 2

Your blueprint

We write your Personal Performance Blueprint. Not a routine, a strategy: fuelling, sleep architecture, recovery, movement and cognitive load, designed around your data and your calendar.

Months 3–11

Recalibration

A steady cadence of strategy sessions, in person or virtual, with micro-corrections between them. Your wearable and glucose data keep the plan honest against your real trajectory, not a snapshot.

Month 6

Mid-year retest

Key markers are re-measured, so progress is verified rather than assumed, and the second half of the year is built on where you actually are.

Month 12

Verified results

A final readout against your January baselines, and your next-year operating plan. You leave with proof, not impressions.

The four levers

Your body is a system, not a mystery.

Everything in the advisory works through four interdependent levers. Each one is recalibrated from your data, and together they decide how consistently you can perform, recover and lead.

Lever one

Mindset

The cognitive patterns behind decision speed, stress response and consistency under pressure.

Lever two

Lifestyle

Daily rhythm, sleep architecture and recovery, structured to stabilise energy across the week.

Lever three

Nutrition

Fuelling and supplementation aligned with your metabolic profile and glucose dynamics.

Lever four

Movement

Strength, mobility and conditioning matched to your physiology and your leadership load.
